# Research Output Format

Use this structure for `research.md`.

```markdown
# Research: <article title>

## Original claims
- Claim or argument from the article
- Another claim worth checking

## Verified or supported
- Finding
  Source: [Title](https://example.com)
  Relevance: why it matters

## Contested, weak, or unclear
- Claim
  Status: what remains uncertain
  Source: [Title](https://example.com)

## Missing context
- What the article leaves out

## Takeaways
- Short, practical synthesis

## Sources
1. [Title](https://example.com)
2. [Title](https://example.com)
```

Rules:

- Keep original claims separate from outside research.
- Cite every external source with a URL.
- Prefer concise, high-signal notes over long prose.
