## Feedback / process correction (notes triage)

- Nisch feedback: my notes triage was too aggressive — I moved personal ideas into **Fence HQ** and converted them into **Tasks** when they should live in **LifeOS → Future Ideas**.
- New rule: **Default to “Idea capture”** for note items unless there is explicit intent, owner, scope, and near-term execution. Only create Tasks when:
  1) the note is clearly about an active project, AND
  2) there’s a concrete next action, AND
  3) it belongs in the correct HQ (Fence HQ only for Fence).
- When in doubt: create an Idea page with a short summary + suggested next steps, and leave it un-tasked.
